```

â–¡ Pay-As-You-Go
  $0.01/example
  100k examples = $1,000

  $99 for 10k examples

```

**Why it works:**
- Discovery Pack makes Acceleration look amazing
- 50% off sounds incredible vs 1% off
- Middle option pushed by poor middle choice

### The Anchoring Effect

**Always show:**
1. Regular pay-as-you-go price (anchor)
2. Savings vs this price (looks huge)
3. Effective rate per example (seems cheap)

**Example:**
```
100,000 examples

Acceleration Pack: $499

YOU SAVE: $501 (50%)
Effective rate: $0.00499 per example
```

### The "Too Good to Pass Up" Effect

**Frame it as:**
- Limited time (urgency)
- Limited quantity (scarcity)
- Never available again (FOMO)
- Exclusive access (status)
